# Design Style Guide - Wool Sneakers E-commerce

## Design Philosophy
**"Natural Wool Meets Urban Tech"** - A sophisticated fusion of organic warmth and contemporary minimalism that celebrates the inherent beauty of natural materials while embracing modern functionality.

## Color Palette
**Primary Colors:**
- **Warm White** (#FEFCF8) - Main background, creates soft, airy feeling
- **Oat** (#E8DCC0) - Secondary backgrounds, subtle warmth
- **Mist Grey** (#B8B5AE) - Text, borders, subtle accents

**Accent Colors:**
- **Charcoal** (#3A3835) - Primary text, strong contrast
- **Sage** (#A8B5A0) - Interactive elements, buttons
- **Cream** (#F5F2E8) - Card backgrounds, subtle elevation

**Gradient Combinations:**
- Warm White → Oat (horizontal gradients for hero sections)
- Mist Grey → Sage (subtle button hover states)
- Oat → Cream (card elevation effects)

## Typography
**Display Font:** Canela (serif) - For headings, brand elements, hero text
**Body Font:** Suisse Int'l (sans-serif) - For body text, navigation, UI elements
**Accent Font:** Neue Haas Grotesk (sans-serif) - For technical specs, product details

## Visual Language
**Material Focus:** Emphasize the softness and texture of wool through:
- Subtle texture overlays on backgrounds
- Soft drop shadows with warm undertones
- Organic, flowing shapes and curves
- Natural fiber-inspired patterns

**Urban Tech Elements:**
- Clean geometric layouts
- Precise grid systems
- Minimal UI components
- Subtle tech-inspired animations

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Used Libraries & Effects:
1. **Anime.js** - Smooth micro-interactions, button hover animations
2. **Pixi.js** - Wool particle effects in hero section
3. **ECharts.js** - Sustainability metrics visualization
4. **Splide.js** - Product image carousels
5. **Typed.js** - Typewriter effect for brand messaging
6. **Splitting.js** - Text reveal animations
7. **p5.js** - Organic wool texture generation

### Animation & Interaction:
- **Text Effects:** Typewriter animation for hero headlines, subtle character stagger on scroll
- **Image Effects:** Ken Burns pan/zoom on hero images, displacement hover reveals
- **Background:** Subtle wool particle system, organic flowing gradients
- **Product Cards:** 3D tilt on hover, soft shadow expansion, image zoom
- **Buttons:** Color morphing from mist grey to sage, gentle glow edges

### Header & Navigation:
- Clean, minimal navigation bar with subtle transparency
- Warm white background with soft shadow
- Typography-focused design with generous whitespace
- Smooth scroll-triggered animations

### Layout & Spacing:
- Generous whitespace to emphasize content quality
- Organic grid systems that feel natural yet structured
- Asymmetrical layouts balanced with consistent spacing
- Mobile-first responsive design with fluid typography scaling

### Product Photography Style:
- Clean, minimal product shots against neutral backgrounds
- Lifestyle photography with natural lighting
- Focus on texture and material details
- Consistent color grading with warm, muted tones

### Interactive Elements:
- Subtle hover states with wool-inspired animations
- Smooth transitions between states
- Tactile feedback through micro-animations
- Consistent interaction patterns throughout

## Brand Personality
**Sophisticated** - Premium materials and refined aesthetics
**Natural** - Authentic wool textures and organic shapes  
**Innovative** - Modern tech integration with traditional materials
**Comfortable** - Soft, approachable, and inviting design
**Sustainable** - Eco-conscious visual messaging and authentic materials
